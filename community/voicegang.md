# VoiceGang

In order to better support musicians from all over the world, VoiceStreet invited 100 KOLs to join the League in the first batch.

#### Requirements:

Love music and digital currency, have more than 10,000 Twitter or telegram followers.

#### Rights and benefits:

* The first 100 KOLs will receive DPGU certification, will become a member of the DPGU Club, will have a VIP icon on their avatar, and the right to re-create DPGU IP.
* Free lifetime tickets for metaverse concert and livehouse held by VoiceStreet
* Priority to participate online and offline private parties held by VoiceStreet musicians
* They will have the rights to customize their exclusive metaverse space
* They can become the first batch of people to access the music games, and can invite other players to enter the metaverse. They can also obtain 5% of the income generated by the invitee in the game through p2e and w2e.
